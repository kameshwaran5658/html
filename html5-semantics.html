HTML5 MathML <semantics> Tag

The <semantics> element is used to provide additional semantic information about a MathML expression. It allows for embedding metadata or annotations that enhance the interpretation of mathematical content.

Basic Usage

<math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML">
    <semantics>
        <mrow>
            <mi>f</mi>
            <mo>(

Attributes

  • encoding: Specifies the encoding used for the embedded annotation, such as LaTeX or MathML.

Conclusion

The <semantics> tag enhances the representation of mathematical content by including additional annotations or metadata, improving accessibility and interpretation.
