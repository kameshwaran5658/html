HTML Title Attribute

The title attribute provides additional information about an element. The information is most often shown as a tooltip when the mouse moves over the element.

Example

<p title="Tooltip text">Hover over this paragraph.</p>

Usage

The title attribute can be used with any HTML element to provide a tooltip.

Conclusion

The title attribute enhances user experience by providing additional context or information.
